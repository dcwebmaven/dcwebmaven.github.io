What We Do

The Commerce Data Service collaborates with the 12 Bureaus of the U.S. Department of Commerce
and private sector partners to improve data product delivery from science to society.

Data Engineering

Engineering, delivery and consulting on Open Data and API projects involving integration, analysis, visualization and increased utilization.

Data Science

Transforming raw data into insights, products, and applications to empower data-driven decision making.

Data Education

Providing employees with data science skills for the information age through the Commerce Data Academy.

Collaborators

In late 2016, Commerce issued an open challenge to build "last mile" solutions that democratize our data and make it accessible to new audiences. These companies accepted the challenge by importing Commerce data into their platforms, making it easier to view, query, analyze and understand. Commerce invites other companies to join our group of creative collaborators.
